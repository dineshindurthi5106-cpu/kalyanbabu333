Konidela Pawan Kalyan is an Indian politician, actor, serving as the 11th Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh since, June 2024.

Born: 2 September 1971 (age 53 years), Bapatla
Upcoming movie: They Call Him OG
Spouse: Anna Lezhneva (2013), Renu Desai (2009–2012), Nandini (1997–2008)
Party: Janasena Party
Children:4 Menmbers
Siblings: Chiranjeevi, Nagendra Babu, Madhavi Rao, Vijaya Durga
Ocuupation:Actor - Politician .

As an actor, Kalyan is known for his distinctive style and mannerisms in Telugu cinema,Popuplarly Known as PSPK.

Key Points:

  • He is among the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list multiple times since 2012.

  • Kalyan holds a black belt in Karate.
